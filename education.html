Education

I am more interested with microprocessors; the core electronics behind these processors: the “chips”, latest evolutions in electronics amazed me. Hence engineering with Electronics as my major was the first choice for my undergraduate studies and I decided to take up Electronics & Communication Engineering (EC) at Charotar Institute Of Technology, Changa(CITC), a reputed college affiliated with GUJARAT TECHNOLOGICAL UNIVERSITY.

During my graduation, I acquired deep knowledge in Basic electronics, Digital Logic design, Data communication, RF communication and Antenna design, Microprocessor & Interfacing, and came to know what an Embedded Systems was.In my final year project, I worked in a team at MASIBUS AUTOMATION & INSTRUMENTATION PVT. LTD and we made “8 Channel Temperature Scanner” using PSoC-3. Out of deep interest in automation, I made RF based home automation system that could control electronic appliances through RF remote.
